Blue is a color that sits between green and purple on the color wheel. It is often linked to calmness, peace, and stability, reminding people of the sky and the ocean. Many see blue as a symbol of trust, honesty, and confidence. Light shades of blue can feel soft and gentle, like a clear morning sky, while darker shades, such as navy, can feel strong and serious. Whether in nature, art, or clothing, blue brings a sense of balance and calm wherever it appears.
